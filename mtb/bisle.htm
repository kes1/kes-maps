Black Isle woods

10 miles

This is a fairly easy going flat cycle through the 5000 hectare forest which runs down the centre of the Black isle. The route is waymarked so navigation is easy, you start from below the Mount Eagle TV mast which can be seen for miles around, although it is a tad more difficult to get to, on the OS landranger sheet the mast is at NH640580. To get there you can either take the first right onto a single track road after Culbokie leaving the A9 at Duncanston, or go via Munlochy, taking the minor road directly across from the B9161 (from the A9) then take the first left and straight on at the crossroads.

Once in the woods, just follow the posts with a wee bike on them, it is fairly well signposted. One particular section of the route can get a bit jaggy with gorse bushes, but this can either be avoided or just wear thick trousers. These woods are full of paths, so I recommend you just explore them for a day (take some pieces).
